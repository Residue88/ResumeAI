About the role
We are seeking an experienced Senior Data Quality Expert to join our team and drive the implementation of our data governance strategy. This role requires a deep understanding of data governance principles, strong leadership skills, and the ability to drive a culture of data stewardship and literacy across the organization.

Your Key Responsibilities
•     Data Quality Monitoring and Analysis: Regularly monitor data quality metrics, identify data discrepancies, and perform root cause analysis to ensure data accuracy and consistency across systems.
•     Data Cleansing: Implement and maintain data cleansing processes to ensure data in-tegrity, including deduplication, standardization, and error correction.
•     Collaboration with Stakeholders: Work closely with data stewards, data owners, and other stakeholders to define and enforce data quality standards and best practices.
•     Reporting and Documentation: Create detailed data quality reports, document data is-sues, and track resolutions. Maintain documentation of data quality procedures and standards.
•     Data Governance Support: Support the data governance framework by ensuring ad-herence to data quality policies and standards across the organization.

Qualifications/competencies:
•     Proven experience in data governance, with a focus on implementing business-centric, value based governance strategies.
•     Strong understanding of data governance principles, data management practices, and data quality frameworks.
•     Experience in building and managing data stewardship programs and fostering a data-driven culture.
•     Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, Statistics, or a re-lated field.
•     Minimum of 3 years of experience in data quality management, data analysis, or a similar role.
•     Analytical mindset with the ability to monitor and measure the effectiveness of data governance initiatives.

Technical Skills:
•     Data Quality Tools: Proficiency in data quality tools such as Informatica Data Quali-ty, Talend, or Trillium.
•     Data Analysis Tools: Experience with SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools like Tableau or Power BI.
•     Database Management: Familiarity with relational databases (SQL Server) and big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark).
•     Programming: Basic knowledge of programming languages like Python for data ma-nipulation and analysis.

Preferred Qualifications:
